About Natalia

I am passionate about the world around me; I am continuously amazed by the potential it holds. Within it are endless opportunities to grow and soak up new information, and evolve as new technologies become available. My enthusiasm for information and my thirst for knowledge leads me to the conclusion that I will only stop seeking new and more when I am done living altogether – and it was for t hese reasons I decided to take part in General Assembly’s 12-week intensive Web Development immersive program. Even in spite of unforeseen hurdles, and some interesting personal challenges, it has been one of the most rewarding and beneficial experiences so far collected for myself.

Since the program's completion I have a more complete understanding of front-end development, and excitement at the creative potential I see inside myself. I enjoy the process of solving problems, and becoming a developer has afforded me the outlet of solving real-time problems with an actual end-user effect. Seeing how different pieces are built and ultimately fit together, from idea conception through product deliverable, is fascinating, challenging, and beyond all, rewarding. This process, regardless of whether I create alone or as part of a team, and all of its elements drives me; my unique perspective of the world and my own personal ambition inspires me; my knowledge and drive to continue learning allows me to put some portion of myself into each project I touch.

Where I came from, Where I want to go

Coming from a background of working with children with dissabilities the desire and passion to help those in need has never vanished. Prior to coming to General Assembly I got the first taste of technology playing into my past career by taking part in a project which would show me that through technology there could be so much help for people dealing with those various struggles. It gave me the courage to leave my path behind while still feeling that I had the tools help a variety of walks of life, from the forunate, to the less so. Although this was the element and passion which brought me here, it is not the only future I see. I am excited to broaden my horizons and to experience the different paths at my fingertips.

General Assembly cemented this notiion. It gave me the tools which enabled me to add to the knowledge I gained on my own and added an entire separate toolkit. I am excited to get a job which will allow me the space and opportunity to continue to grow and learn. What I have learned so far is just the beginning, and that is one of the best feelings. The potential for growth and knowledge is never ending. Its hands down the most inspiring and driving force. I can't wait to find a place that I can use these skills to benefit them and add to my knowledge in the process
